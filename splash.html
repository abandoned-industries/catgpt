CatGPT

Unofficial ChatGPT desktop wrapper

Unofficial desktop wrapper for ChatGPT.com. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI.

Before you log in

  1. Choose Continue with Google.
  2. If Google shows Use your passkey, do NOT press Continue — passkeys cannot work inside this app.
  3. Click Try another way and use a phone prompt, authenticator code, or SMS instead.
  4. This is one-time; your session persists across restarts.
