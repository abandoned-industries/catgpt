CatGPT

Unofficial ChatGPT desktop wrapper

Unofficial desktop wrapper for ChatGPT.com. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI. CatGPT itself collects no data — no analytics, no telemetry; everything you type goes directly to ChatGPT.com.

Before you log in

  1. Choose Continue with Google — the only tested sign-in path.
  2. Expect a passkey screen twice: first Google's (Use your passkey), then — after Google succeeds — OpenAI's own (Verify your identity). Passkeys cannot work inside this app; never pick them.
  3. Both times take the alternative: Try another way (Google — e.g. text message, or tap Yes on your phone) and Try another method (OpenAI — e.g. push notification to your ChatGPT mobile app, then approve there).
  4. This is one-time; your session persists across restarts.
